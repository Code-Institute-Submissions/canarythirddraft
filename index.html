What is this weird hippy metal?

Canary are a brisbane based, dark poppy grunge band that like deep grooves with heavy overtones. We play gigs locally in bars and pubs and have also been hired for house parties. We don't do pub rock and we don't do "classics". We perform our own style of catchy dark tones and provoking lyrics. It's good to dance to, it's good to drink to and it's good to listen too.

Get in touch if you are a venue looking for original bands or if you are hosting an event and would like to secure us as entertainment.
